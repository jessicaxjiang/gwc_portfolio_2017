biography

I am a rising junior at Julia R. Masterman Laboratory and Demonstration School. My family moved to the United States in the mid 1990s from FuZhou, FuJian, China to New York then Philadelphia (where I was borned). I have always wanted to work in the STEM field but I am not quite sure which at the moment. At my high school, I am the stage manager for the annual musical (also, co-captain of stage crew). I am deeply involved with an international volunteer organization called Youth Volunteer Corps; Currently, I am on my local advisory board and just recently been accepted into the International Youth Advisory Board (IYAB). Also, I am on my school's math team and badminton team. Occasionally, I edit videos for fun.
P.S I am NOT a morning person and WILL NOT be awake before noon unless I REALLY have to be for whatever reason. ^-^

Random Fact: I am the first in my extended family to be born in the United States of America.
